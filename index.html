Germinate Delay – Sound that grows like nature

Germinate is not just another delay plugin – it's an organic ecosystem of echoes. Inspired by the growth of seeds into sprouts, it combines warmth, unpredictability, and movement into a living texture.

Soft Mode – Smooth and pure, with chorus-like modulation and subtle stereo widening. Perfect for clean ambience and natural shimmer.

Hard Mode – Complex and evolving, featuring multi-rate LFOs, resonant filtering, stereo spread, and breathing modulation. Built for rich, dynamic echoes that bloom and pulse with life.

Every parameter feels alive: Sprout Time for delay, Loopback for feedback, Water for mix, Breeze for modulation, and Sunlight for tone. From tape-like warmth to futuristic textures, Germinate Delay grows sound into something entirely new.
